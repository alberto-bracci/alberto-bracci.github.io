Biography

I am a physicist interested in complex systems. My main focus is the study of social systems and human behavior, by means of complex networks, data science and mathematical modelling. My current focus is the study of darknet marketplaces, amazon-like platforms on the deep web where people trade mostly illicit goods in a trustless environment.

Currently, I am a Phd student in mathematics at City, University of London, based in the department of Mathematics under the supervision of Dr. Andrea Baronchelli.

Previously, I was a research intern at the Sony Computer Science Lab in Paris, where I carried out my thesis for the International Master in Physics of Complex systems on Opinion Dynamics.
